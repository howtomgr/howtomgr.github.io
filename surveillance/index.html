Shinobi Installation Guide

Shinobi is a free and open-source CCTV/NVR (Network Video Recorder) solution written in Node.js. It serves as a modern FOSS alternative to proprietary video surveillance systems like Blue Iris, Milestone XProtect, or commercial NVR solutions, supporting RTSP, ONVIF, motion detection, and multi-user access.
